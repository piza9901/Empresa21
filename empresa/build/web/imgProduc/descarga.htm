    Product Description

    BL-6262 Dual Ball Bearing Turbocharger  

    Billet Compressor Wheel | 1 Year Warranty | 750HP Power Rating

    Description

    This turbo features a Billet 62mm compressor wheel and a 62mm Turbine for quick spool. This unit comes standard with a ported 4" Inlet / 2.5" Outlet compressor housing. A Dual Ball Bearing oil and water cooled CHRA designed to handle high boost operation with ease, water cooling must be used with this turbocharger. The Boost Lab Billet 6262 is fully assembled and VSR balanced in-house. All of our BL turbochargers come with an industry standard 1 year unlimited mileage warranty.  This unit is in stock and ships same day.

    Specs:

    • Compressor Housing: Ported 4" Inlet / 2.5" Outlet
    • Compressor Wheel: Billet 11-Blade 62mm  
    • Bearing Type: Oil & Water Cooled Dual Ball Bearing CHRA (water cooling must be used)
    • Turbine Wheel: Inconel 62mm
    • Oil Feed: 7/16-24" 
    • Water Ports: M14 x 1.5 
    • Warranty: 1 Year/Unlimited Mile

    Turbine Housing Options:

    • T3 .63 A/R w/ 3" 4-Bolt Outlet
    • T3 .82 A/R w/ 3" 4-Bolt Outlet
    • T3 .63 A/R w/ 3" V-Band Outlet
    • T3 .82 A/R w/ 3" V-Band Outlet
    • T4 .82 A/R w/ 3" V-Band Outlet 
    • SS V-BanD In/Out .82 A/R (+275)

    BL Turbocharger Upgrade Options

    We offer a verity of upgrades and modifications for our BL Series turbochargers at an additional cost. Compressor cover coating & polishing, compressor wheel anodizing, T51R compressor cover modification, and turbine housing ceramic coating.
